Data Structure


Data was scrapped from: /www.whatuni.com

Scrapping and loading to MongoDb py file could be found here: /www.github.com

Each scrapped review has the following fields:


1. User Id
2. College name
3. Date of review
4. Overall unoversity rating
5. Text review for overall university rating
6. Job prospects rating
7. Text review for job prospects rating
8. Course and Lectures rating
9. Text review for course and lectures rating
10. Accomodation rating
11. Text review for accomodation rating
12. Uni facilities rating
13. Text review for uni facilities rating
14. City life rating
15. Text review for city life rating
16. Clubs and Societies rating
17. Text review for clubs and societies rating
